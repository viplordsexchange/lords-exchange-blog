   - On the homepage, there is a login dashboard—enter your unique username and password there.
   - Click login, and you will be redirected to your user dashboard account.

Explore your dashboard, where you will see options like games and sports—click on them to check out many games and sports you like to start with. You can also search through the navigation menu.
