## Andar Bahar

Andar Bahar is a simple Indian card game where players predict which side will receive the matching card first.

### How to Play

1. Open Andar Bahar.

## Blackjack

Blackjack is a strategy-based card game where the objective is to reach a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

### How to Play

1. Join a Blackjack table.
2. Enter your preferred amount.
3. Receive your cards.
4. Decide whether to **Hit** or **Stand**.
5. Beat the dealer without exceeding 21.

Blackjack combines decision-making with chance, making it one of the most recognized card games worldwide.
